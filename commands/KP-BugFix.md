Invoke the `KP-BugFix` skill on whatever is broken. Read `~/.claude/skills/KP-BugFix/SKILL.md` and follow the six phases exactly: reproduce, minimise, hypothesise, instrument, fix, regression-check. File a post-mortem to the Obsidian Brain vault when done.
